The Parliament Project

This webpage describes the Greek Parliament project, an attempt to provide indexed information about events regarding the greek parliament and its conferences. It accomplishes that by converting the parliament transcripts from raw text (.txt .doc or .pdf) to Akoma Ntoso.

For visualization and hosting, SayIt and PopIt is be used.

The project is still in development. Attached are some sample original, some converted files and a (very outdated) project report. Keep your eyes open here and on http://greek-parliament.sayit.mysociety.org for updates. We expect at least all the transcripts between 2012 and 2015 to be properly up before election day (Jan 25).
